Baltic Capitals

Three medieval old towns in a row, plus the Curonian Spit sand dunes.

Stops: Tallinn · Lahemaa · Riga · Sigulda · Vilnius · Trakai · Curonian Spit

Temp: 70–75°F, sun until ~10:30 pm

Three medieval old towns in a row — Tallinn, Riga, Vilnius. Sea breezes at the Curonian Spit, late-night sunsets (sun until ~10:30 pm). ~70–75°F. Cruise daytrippers pack the old towns midday — eat early or late.

Thursday: Fly SAN → Tallinn (~16h)

  • United + Finnair
  • Land Friday afternoon

Friday: Arrive Tallinn

  • Hotel Telegraaf or Schlössle
  • Walk old town in long evening light
  • Dinner at Olde Hansa

Saturday: Toompea + old town

  • Toompea viewpoints
  • KGB Museum at Hotel Viru
  • Telliskivi creative district

Sunday: Lahemaa National Park

  • Private driver (~1h each way)
  • Viru Bog boardwalk
  • Altja and Käsmu fishing villages

Monday: Patarei + Kadriorg

  • Patarei Sea Fortress (former Soviet prison)
  • KUMU art museum in Kadriorg Park
  • Sauna at Iglupark

Tuesday: Bus to Riga (~4.5h)

  • LUX Express
  • Pullman Riga or Grand Poet
  • Evening at House of Blackheads

Wednesday: Riga Art Nouveau

  • Alberta iela facades
  • Central Market in old zeppelin hangars
  • Lunch at Milda

Thursday: Sigulda + Cēsis

  • Train ~1h to Sigulda
  • Turaida Castle + Gauja gondola
  • 30 min on to Cēsis castle ruins

Friday: Riga → Vilnius via Hill of Crosses

  • LUX Express, ~6h with stop
  • Hill of Crosses at Šiauliai
  • Hotel Pacai in Vilnius
  • Evening in Užupis

Saturday: Old Vilnius

  • Cathedral Square + Gediminas Tower
  • Bernardinai Gardens
  • Dinner at Lokys

Sunday: Trakai Castle

  • 30 min drive or train
  • Lake-island brick castle
  • Kibinai pastries at Kybynlar

Monday: Curonian Spit

  • Vilnius → Klaipėda (~3.5h), ferry, drive to Nida
  • Parnidis Dune + Thomas Mann's summer house
  • Could overnight in Nida if you'd rather slow down

Tuesday: Fly home from Vilnius

  • Last cafés like Crooked House
  • Afternoon flight
