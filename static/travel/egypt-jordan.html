Egypt + Jordan

Pyramids, Nile cruise, Petra by candlelight, Wadi Rum stargazing.

Stops: Cairo · Luxor · Aswan · Amman · Petra · Wadi Rum

Temp: 60–80°F

Day 1: Fly SAN → Cairo (~22h via Frankfurt or Istanbul)

StayMarriott Mena House

Day 2: Pyramids of Giza

  • Sunrise at the plateau before the buses arrive
  • Khufu's pyramid interior
  • Lunch at 9 Pyramids Lounge

Day 3: Cairo — Egyptian Museum + Old Cairo

  • Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) opened 2025
  • Hanging Church + Khan el-Khalili bazaar
  • Dinner at Abou El Sid

Day 4: Fly to Luxor

StaySofitel Winter Palace

  • East Bank: Karnak + Luxor Temple
  • Hot air balloon over the Valley of the Kings at dawn (next morning)

Day 5: West Bank Luxor

  • Valley of the Kings (Tutankhamun, Ramses VI)
  • Hatshepsut's mortuary temple
  • Colossi of Memnon

Day 6: Nile cruise to Aswan

  • Board Sanctuary Sun Boat IV or Oberoi Zahra
  • Edfu and Kom Ombo temples en route

Day 7: Aswan + Abu Simbel

  • Pre-dawn drive or flight to Abu Simbel
  • Ramses II's colossal temple
  • Felucca on the Nile at sunset

Day 8: Fly Cairo → Amman

StayFour Seasons Amman

  • Connection in Cairo

Day 9: Drive to Petra (~3h)

StayMövenpick at the gates

  • Petra by Night candlelight walk

Day 10: Petra — full day

  • Down the Siq to the Treasury at dawn
  • Hike to the Monastery (~800 steps up)
  • Sunset viewpoint above the Treasury

Day 11: Drive to Wadi Rum

StayMemories Aicha Luxury Camp

  • Jeep tour through Lawrence of Arabia's desert
  • Stargazing at the camp

Day 12: Drive back to Amman + fly home

  • Stop at Dead Sea for a float
  • Late flight home
