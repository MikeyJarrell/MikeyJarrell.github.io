Canadian Rockies

Lake Louise, Moraine Lake, and the Icefields Parkway drive to Jasper.

Stops: Banff · Lake Louise · Icefields Parkway · Jasper

Temp: 55–75°F

Day 1: Fly SAN → Calgary (~4h)

  • Drive to Banff (~1.5h)
  • Fairmont Banff Springs

Day 2: Banff — Sulphur + Lake Minnewanka

  • Gondola up Sulphur Mountain
  • Boat tour on Lake Minnewanka
  • Banff Avenue for dinner

Day 3: Moraine Lake + Lake Louise

  • Arrive Moraine before sunrise (very limited parking — book shuttle)
  • Lake Louise canoe rental
  • Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise for tea

Day 4: Drive Icefields Parkway to Jasper

StayJasper Park Lodge

  • Bow Lake + Peyto Lake stops
  • Columbia Icefield SkyWalk

Day 5: Jasper — Maligne Lake

  • Boat to Spirit Island
  • Wildlife along the road — elk, bighorn sheep

Day 6: Mt. Edith Cavell + Athabasca Falls

  • Cavell Meadows hike (~5mi)
  • Wave-carved Athabasca Falls
  • Back to Jasper for dinner

Day 7: Drive back to Calgary + home

  • Bow Valley scenic route
  • Late flight home
