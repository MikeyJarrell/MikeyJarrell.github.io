South Africa

Cape Town wine country, the Garden Route, and a Big-5 Kruger safari.

Stops: Cape Town · Stellenbosch · Hermanus · Garden Route · Kruger

Temp: 55–80°F

Day 1: Fly SAN → Cape Town (~22h via Doha or Frankfurt)

StayEllerman House on the Atlantic Seaboard

  • Sunset on Camps Bay

Day 2: Cape Town — Table Mountain + city

  • Cable car up Table Mountain (early to beat clouds)
  • Bo-Kaap colorful houses
  • Dinner at La Colombe

Day 3: Cape Peninsula day

  • Drive to Cape Point
  • Boulders Beach penguins
  • Lunch at Two Oceans

Day 4: Stellenbosch wine country (~1h)

StayBabylonstoren or Delaire Graff Estate

  • Lunch at Jordan Restaurant
  • Back to Cape Town

Day 5: Drive the Garden Route — Hermanus (~1.5h)

StayBirkenhead House

  • Whale-watching from Walker Bay (Jun–Nov)

Day 6: Drive to Plettenberg Bay (~5h)

StayThe Plettenberg or Forest Lodge

  • Robberg peninsula walk (~2.5mi)

Day 7: Tsitsikamma forest + drive to Knysna

  • Storms River suspension bridge
  • Knysna oysters for dinner

Day 8: Fly Cape Town → Kruger area

StaySingita Lebombo or Royal Malewane

  • Onward to Skukuza or Hoedspruit

Day 9: Kruger — full safari day

  • Sunrise and afternoon game drives
  • Big-5 sightings — leopard, lion, rhino, elephant, buffalo

Day 10: Kruger — full safari day

  • Walking safari with armed ranger
  • Bush dinner under stars

Day 11: Kruger — full safari day

  • Optional helicopter flight over the bushveld
  • Sundowner at a sunset viewpoint

Day 12: Fly back to Cape Town

  • Last shopping at the V&A Waterfront
  • Dinner at Test Kitchen Fledgelings

Day 13: Fly home

  • Overnight flight to SAN
