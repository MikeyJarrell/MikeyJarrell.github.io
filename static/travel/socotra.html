Socotra

Dragon's blood trees, alien endemics, white-sand coves on Yemen's lost-island Eden.

Stops: Hadibo · Firmhin plateau · Detwah Lagoon · Hoq Cave · Arher

Temp: 75–90°F, dry season

Visa: Yemen, currently obtained via tour operator on arrival in Cairo or Abu Dhabi (charter flights). Confirm requirements 2 months ahead.

Day 1: Fly SAN → Abu Dhabi (~16h)

  • Overnight in AUH
  • Yas Marina hotel near the airport

Day 2: Charter to Hadibo

  • Weekly charter flight AUH → Socotra (~3h)
  • Meet tour operator, transfer to camp
  • Sunset at Dihamri marine reserve

Day 3: Dragon's Blood forest

StayCamp

  • Drive to Firmhin plateau
  • Hike among the Dracaena cinnabari — only place on Earth

Day 4: Wadi Dirhur + Shibahon viewpoint

StayCamp

  • Cool freshwater swimming holes
  • Drive to Shibahon for the dragon-tree panorama

Day 5: Detwah Lagoon

StayCamp

  • White-sand lagoon and dune snorkel
  • Spend the night with the Bedouin cave family

Day 6: Hoq Cave

StayCamp

  • 3-hour stalactite cave hike (~3mi)
  • Beach afternoon at Arher

Day 7: Archer Beach + Erissel Point

StayCamp on the dunes

  • Easternmost point of the island

Day 8: Hadibo + charter back

StayCamp on the dunes

  • Souk in Hadibo for frankincense
  • Charter to Abu Dhabi
  • Long flight home
