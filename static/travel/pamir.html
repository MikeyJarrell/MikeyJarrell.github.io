Pamir Highway

Yurt camps and 4WD across one of the world's most remote highways.

Stops: Bishkek · Song-Köl · Osh · Murghab · Wakhan · Dushanbe

Temp: 50–80°F (high-altitude)

Pamir Highway (Kyrgyzstan + Tajikistan)

Day 1: Fly SAN → Bishkek (~22h via Istanbul)

  • Novotel Bishkek
  • Ala-Too Square evening walk

Day 2: Bishkek + drive to Song-Köl prep

  • Osh Bazaar
  • Drive south toward the Tian Shan
  • Overnight in Kochkor

Day 3: Drive to Song-Köl lake

  • Yurt camp at 10,000 ft
  • Horseback ride on the jailoo (summer pasture)

Day 4: Song-Köl — full day

  • Eagle hunter demonstration (occasional)
  • Hike to the lake's edge

Day 5: Drive to Tash Rabat

  • 15th-century caravanserai in the mountains
  • Yurt camp nearby

Day 6: Drive to Osh (~6h)

  • Sulayman Mountain
  • Bazaar afternoon

Day 7: Cross into Tajikistan — Sary Tash

  • Border crossing at Kyzyl-Art (4,280 m)
  • Pamir mountain views
  • Overnight Sary Tash homestay

Day 8: Drive to Murghab

  • Highest town in Tajikistan (~3,650 m)
  • Pamir Hotel Murghab

Day 9: Drive to Bulunkul + Wakhan corridor

  • Lake Bulunkul — coldest inhabited spot in CIS
  • Yamchun fortress overlooking Afghanistan
  • Hot springs at Bibi Fatima

Day 10: Wakhan Valley

  • Vrang petroglyphs
  • Pamir-Wakhan rivers confluence
  • Homestay in Langar

Day 11: Drive to Khorog

  • Tajik–Afghan border ride along the Panj river
  • Serena Inn Khorog

Day 12: Drive to Dushanbe (~14h or fly)

  • Long drive day — or charter flight
  • Hyatt Regency Dushanbe

Day 13: Dushanbe rest day

  • National Museum of Antiquities
  • Massive flagpole

Day 14: Fly home from Dushanbe

  • Via Istanbul, long return
