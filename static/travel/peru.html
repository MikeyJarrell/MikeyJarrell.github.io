Peru

Machu Picchu at dawn, Lima's food scene, the Sacred Valley, Amazon canoeing.

Stops: Lima · Cusco · Sacred Valley · Machu Picchu · Puerto Maldonado

Temp: 40–75°F (varies by altitude)

Day 1: Fly SAN → Lima (~9h)

StayHotel B

  • Dinner at Maido (one of the world's top restaurants)

Day 2: Lima

StayHotel B

  • Barranco art walk
  • Lunch at Central (#1 in the world some years)
  • Larco Museum

Day 3: Fly to Cusco (~1.5h)

StayBelmond Hotel Monasterio

  • Slow afternoon — Cusco is 11,150 ft, acclimate
  • Coca tea

Day 4: Sacred Valley — Pisac + Ollantaytambo

StaySol y Luna Lodge

  • Pisac ruins in the morning
  • Ollantaytambo fortress + town walk

Day 5: Maras + Moray + Salt Mines

StaySol y Luna Lodge

  • Concentric circular terraces at Moray
  • Maras salt pans on the hillside
  • Cooking class in the valley

Day 6: Train to Machu Picchu

StayBelmond Sanctuary Lodge

  • PeruRail Vistadome from Ollantaytambo
  • Late-afternoon entry to beat crowds

Day 7: Machu Picchu sunrise + Huayna Picchu hike

StayBelmond Sanctuary Lodge

  • First bus up at 5:30am
  • Hike Huayna Picchu for the over-the-shoulder view (~1.5mi, steep)
  • Train back to Cusco

Day 8: Choquequirao trek (or rest day in Cusco)

StayBelmond Sanctuary Lodge

  • Adventurous option: 4-day trek to a quieter Inca city
  • Easier: explore Cusco and San Pedro market

Day 9: Fly to Puerto Maldonado

StayInkaterra Reserva Amazónica

  • Amazon rainforest gateway
  • Night walk for caimans

Day 10: Amazon — Lake Sandoval

StayInkaterra Reserva Amazónica

  • Canoe on the oxbow lake
  • Giant otters, hoatzin birds, capybaras

Day 11: Fly back to Lima + home

StayInkaterra Reserva Amazónica

  • Cusco → Lima
  • Late dinner at Astrid y Gastón
