New Zealand

Mostly South Island — fjords, glaciers, Tongariro volcanoes, Queenstown adrenaline.

Stops: Auckland · Rotorua · Tongariro · Mt. Cook · Queenstown · Milford Sound

Temp: 50–75°F, southern summer
