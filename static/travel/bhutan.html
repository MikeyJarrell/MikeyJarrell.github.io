Bhutan

Himalayan monasteries, the Tiger's Nest hike, and a country with almost no tourists.

Stops: Paro · Thimphu · Punakha

Temp: 50–70°F

Sustainable Development Fee: $200/day required for foreign visitors. Tours arranged through licensed operators only.

Day 1: Fly SAN → Singapore (~17h)

  • Overnight at Crowne Plaza Changi Airport

Day 2: Fly to Paro (only via Bangkok or Delhi or Singapore)

StayAmankora Paro or Six Senses Paro

  • Bhutan Airlines or DrukAir — one of the world's most dramatic landings

Day 3: Paro Valley

  • Rinpung Dzong fortress-monastery
  • National Museum at Ta Dzong
  • Easy walk through the rice paddies

Day 4: Tiger's Nest Monastery

  • 5-mile round-trip hike up to Paro Taktsang, the cliffside monastery (~700m gain)
  • Lunch at the halfway café with the best view

Day 5: Drive to Thimphu (~1h)

StaySix Senses Thimphu

  • Buddha Dordenma giant statue
  • Tashichho Dzong at dusk

Day 6: Thimphu — culture day

  • National Memorial Chorten morning prayers
  • Folk Heritage Museum
  • Painting school

Day 7: Drive to Punakha (~3h)

StayAmankora Punakha

  • Dochula Pass with 108 chortens
  • Punakha Dzong at the river confluence

Day 8: Punakha Valley

  • Chimi Lhakhang fertility temple
  • Suspension bridge over the Mo Chhu
  • Rafting on the river

Day 9: Drive back to Paro, fly out

  • Last morning at the dzong
  • Evening flight, connection home
