Slovenia

Alpine lakes, turquoise rivers, and an island finish on Hvar.

Stops: Ljubljana · Lake Bled · Soča Valley · Piran · Hvar Temp: 75–80°F

Off-radar Alpine charm. Lake Bled island, Ljubljana old town, Postojna caves, Soča River valley — pairs easily with a Croatian island. ~75–80°F.

Thursday: Fly SAN → Ljubljana (~14h)

  • United/Lufthansa
  • Land Friday morning

Friday: Arrive Ljubljana

  • InterContinental or Vander Hotel
  • Walk the Ljubljanica river
  • Dinner at Pri Vitezu

Saturday: Explore old Ljubljana

  • Castle funicular + Tivoli Park
  • Open Kitchen Saturday market
  • Dragon Bridge + Triple Bridge

Sunday: Postojna Cave day trip

  • Cave train + walking tour (or swap for the smaller, quieter Škocjan Caves UNESCO)
  • Cliff-built Predjama Castle
  • Back to Ljubljana

Monday: Drive to Lake Bled (~1h)

  • Vila Bled or Hotel Triglav
  • Pletna boat to island church
  • Lake loop walk (~4mi flat)
  • Overnight at Bled (night 1 of 2)

Tuesday: Vintgar Gorge + Lake Bohinj

  • Vintgar boardwalk (~1mi)
  • Lake Bohinj + Savica waterfall
  • Cream cake at Park Hotel
  • Second night at Bled (catch dawn light over the island)

Wednesday: Drive Soča Valley

  • Vršič Pass scenic drive
  • Soča River swim or kayak (turquoise gorge water)
  • Tolmin Gorges + Kobarid WWI museum
  • Overnight Hotel Hvala

Thursday: Drive to Piran (~3h)

  • Walk the city walls
  • Seafood at Cantina Klet
  • Sunset on the breakwater

Friday: Drive to Ljubljana + fly to Split + ferry to Hvar

  • Drive Piran → Ljubljana airport (~1.5h)
  • Fly Ljubljana → Split (~1h)
  • Catamaran ferry Split → Hvar (~1h)
  • Hotel Park Hvar

Saturday: Pakleni Islands swim day

  • Water-taxi to Palmižana
  • Lunch at Toto's
  • Evening on Hvar's stone streets

Sunday: Explore Hvar interior

  • Stari Grad UNESCO plain
  • Lavender fields above Velo Grablje
  • Beach at Mlini

Monday: Hvar → Ljubljana

  • Ferry Hvar → Split
  • Fly Split → Ljubljana
  • Last evening in Ljubljana

Tuesday: Fly home from Ljubljana

  • Coffee at Le Petit
  • Last walk along the river
  • Afternoon flight
