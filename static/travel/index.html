Tanzania

Tanzania

Big-5 game drives in the northern Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, beach finish on Zanzibar. ~75°F days, ~55°F nights — peak dry season.

Heads up: Mara River crossings typically peak late July through early September. In mid-July (this trip's window) the herds are usually still moving north and crossings are unreliable. You'll see massive herds in the northern Serengeti either way; witnessing an actual crossing is luck of the draw.

Thursday: Fly SAN → Kilimanjaro (~22h)

  • Drive to LAX (~2h)
  • Qatar Airways LAX → JRO, overnight

Friday: Arrive Arusha

  • Land at Kilimanjaro Intl. Airport (JRO)
  • 45-min drive to Onsea House (5-room boutique B&B, garden setting)
  • Briefing dinner with safari guide

Saturday: Fly to Serengeti (~1h)

  • Light aircraft to Seronera (~1h)
  • Lemala Ewanjan tented camp
  • Afternoon game drive

Sunday: Drive to northern Serengeti (~5h)

  • Drive south through Lobo, game-spotting en route
  • Sayari or Lamai camp
  • Watch the Mara for crossings (caveat above)

Monday: Northern Serengeti — full day

  • Cheetah and leopard country
  • Picnic lunch in the bush
  • Sundowners on the Mara

Tuesday: Fly to Ngorongoro (~1h)

  • Light aircraft Sayari → Manyara airstrip
  • Drive to crater rim (~2h)
  • Overnight at Plantation Lodge or The Manor at Ngorongoro

Wednesday: Ngorongoro Crater

  • Pre-dawn descent into the crater
  • Big-tusker elephants and black rhinos
  • Maasai boma visit on the way out
  • Second night at Plantation Lodge / The Manor

Thursday: Fly to Zanzibar (~2h)

  • Light aircraft from Manyara airstrip
  • Emerson Spice rooftop dinner

Friday: Explore Stone Town

  • Old Fort + House of Wonders
  • Slave-market memorial
  • Forodhani night market grills

Saturday: Transfer to Kendwa (~1.5h)

  • Drive north to the coast
  • Kilindi or Zuri Zanzibar
  • Beach + sunset dhow bar

Sunday: Spice farm + sunset dhow

  • Tangawizi Spice Farm tour and lunch
  • Traditional dhow with snorkel stop

Monday: Fly home (~28h)

  • Final swim
  • Qatar Airways ZNZ → LAX

Tuesday: Land San Diego

  • Buffer day at home

Iceland

Iceland

Endless daylight and zero humidity. Ring Road drive, Reynisfjara black sands, Jökulsárlón glacier lagoon, Sky Lagoon soak. ~55–65°F all month.

Thursday: Fly SAN → Reykjavík (~12h)

Friday: Reykjavík + Sky Lagoon

  • Walk Reykjavík harbor
  • Dinner at Matur og Drykkur

Saturday: Drive the Golden Circle

  • Þingvellir, Geysir, Gullfoss
  • Soak at Secret Lagoon
  • Overnight in Hella

Sunday: Drive south coast (~3h)

  • Seljalandsfoss + Skógafoss
  • Reynisfjara black-sand beach
  • Overnight Hotel Kría in Vík

Monday: Skaftafell + Jökulsárlón

  • Skaftafell Svartifoss loop (~2mi flat)
  • Jökulsárlón glacier lagoon boat ride
  • Diamond Beach
  • Overnight Hali Country Hotel or Fosshotel Glacier Lagoon

Tuesday: Drive east to Egilsstaðir (~5h)

  • Stokksnes / Vestrahorn stop
  • Long scenic drive along the east fjords
  • Overnight Egilsstaðir

Wednesday: Mývatn area

  • Mývatn Nature Baths
  • Krafla geothermal area + Goðafoss

  • Long drive south
  • Hotel Búðir or Stykkishólmur

Friday: Snæfellsnes peninsula

  • Kirkjufell viewpoint
  • Arnarstapi cliff walk (~2mi)
  • Búðakirkja black church

Saturday: Drive back to Reykjavík (~3h) + Glymur hike

  • Glymur waterfall hike (~2-3h, second-tallest in Iceland)
  • Borgarnes Settlement Center stop
  • Lunch at Settlement Café

Sunday: Explore Reykjavík

  • Hallgrímskirkja tower
  • Harpa concert hall
  • Fish and chips at Reykjavík Fish

Monday: Landmannalaugar super-jeep day

  • Full-day super-jeep into the highlands (~10-12h)
  • Rhyolite mountains, hot-river soak at Laugavegur trailhead
  • Terrain only accessible by 4WD; pure highland landscape
  • Whale watching from Reykjavík is the rest-day alternative if the jeep tour feels too much

Tuesday: Blue Lagoon, fly home

  • Premium booking with Lava Restaurant lunch
  • Drop rental at Keflavík
  • Afternoon flight

  • Fly Split → Ljubljana
  • Last evening in Ljubljana

Tuesday: Fly home from Ljubljana

  • Coffee at Le Petit

  • Afternoon flight

Albania

Albania

The Adriatic the rest of Europe hasn't found yet. Ksamil and Dhërmi beaches, Ottoman-era Berat and Gjirokastër, Tirana cafés. Heat warning: ~80–85°F coast but inland (Berat / Gjirokastër) routinely 95–100°F in July — plan dawn / late-afternoon walks.

Thursday: Fly SAN → Tirana (~17h)

  • United + Austrian Airlines
  • Land late evening
  • Hotel Plaza Tirana

Friday: Explore Tirana

  • Bunk'art 2 (Cold War bunker museum)
  • Skanderbeg Square + Et'hem Bey Mosque
  • Pyramid of Tirana viewpoint
  • Dinner at Mullixhiu in Blloku

Saturday: Krujë + drive to Berat (~3h with stops)

  • Skanderbeg Castle + Old Bazaar in Krujë (~2h stop on the way)
  • Continue drive south to Berat
  • Hotel Mangalemi inside the white-house quarter
  • Walk Kala castle in evening light
  • Dinner overlooking Mangalem

Sunday: Drive Berat → Gjirokastër (~2h)

  • Onufri Museum morning
  • Lunch at Antipatrea
  • Hotel Cajupi

Monday: Explore Gjirokastër

  • Castle and weapons museum

Tuesday: Drive to Sarandë (~1h)

  • Hotel Bougainville Bay or Demi

Wednesday: Butrint + Ksamil

  • UNESCO Butrint ruins morning

  • Ksamil beach afternoon

Thursday: Ferry Sarandë → Corfu (~30min)

  • Day-trip or overnight on Corfu (recommend overnight for less rushed pace)
  • Corfu Old Town: Liston, Spianada, Old Fortress
  • Dinner at Klimataria Bellos

Friday: Corfu — Achilleion + Paleokastritsa

Saturday: Drive Riviera north (~3h)

Sunday: Dhërmi beach day

  • Drymades Beach loungers

Monday: Drive Llogara Pass → Tirana (~4h)

  • Panorama stop on the pass
  • Drive via Vlorë
  • Back at Hotel Plaza Tirana

Tuesday: Fly home from Tirana

Georgia

Georgia (the country)

Caucasus food, wine, and mountain villages. Tbilisi old town, Gergeti Trinity Church under Mt. Kazbek, Kakheti vineyards, Davit Gareja desert monastery. ~75–85°F.

Thursday: Fly SAN → Tbilisi (~21h)

  • United + Turkish Airlines
  • Land Friday midday

Friday: Arrive Tbilisi + sulfur baths

  • Stamba Hotel or Rooms Tbilisi
  • Sulfur bath at Chreli Abano
  • Dinner at Shavi Lomi

Saturday: Explore old Tbilisi

  • Narikala fortress cable car
  • Mother of Georgia statue
  • Mtatsminda funicular at sunset
  • Khinkali at Zakhar Zakharich

Sunday: Davit Gareja monastery day trip

  • ~2h drive each way to the Azerbaijan border
  • 6th-century cave monastery cut into the cliffs
  • Semi-desert landscape, dramatic and underrated
  • Supra (feast) at Azarphesha back in Tbilisi

Monday: Drive to Kazbegi (~3h) + Gergeti Trinity hike

  • Jvari Pass scenic drive
  • Check in at Rooms Hotel Kazbegi
  • Hike to Gergeti Trinity Church (~4mi RT, ~700m / 2,300ft elevation gain, 4–5h strenuous)
  • Alternative if not feeling the hike: 4WD up the back road in ~30 min

Tuesday: Truso Valley walk

  • Gentle valley walk through ruined towers and mineral springs
  • Permit required at the border police checkpoint (free, on arrival) — this is a Russian border zone
  • OR short Juta hike for higher Caucasus
  • Lunch at Khevsuri

Wednesday: Drive to Kakheti via Mtskheta (~5h)

  • Jvari Monastery + Svetitskhoveli
  • Continue to Sighnaghi
  • Pheasant's Tears for dinner

Thursday: Kakheti wine country

  • Family marani in Telavi
  • Alaverdi Monastery
  • Lunch at Shumi

Friday: More Kakheti, drive to Tbilisi (~2h)

  • Bodbe Monastery morning
  • Twins Wine Cellar
  • Drive back to Tbilisi

Saturday: Fly to Batumi (~1h)

  • Hilton Batumi
  • Evening on the Boulevard
  • Ali & Nino sculpture at sunset

Sunday: Batumi gardens + beach

  • Botanical Gardens loop (~3mi, 9km north of city)
  • Beach at Sheraton
  • Khachapuri Acharuli for dinner

Monday: Fly back to Tbilisi

  • Fabrika district
  • Last khinkali at Sakhli #11

Tuesday: Fly home from Tbilisi

  • Carpet/ceramics at Caravan
  • Lunch at Café Stamba
  • Evening flight

Alternative finale: Svaneti (Mestia + Ushguli) instead of Batumi. Most dramatic mountain landscape in the country, but logistically harder — weather-dependent Mestia flight or 12h drive.

Argentina

Argentina

Southern winter — mild, not cold. Buenos Aires steaks and tango, Mendoza wine country, subtropical Iguazú Falls (~75°F). BA ~55–60°F. Mendoza vines are bare in July (no green canopy) but the Andes backdrop is dramatic.

🚨 Brazil visa required. As of April 2025, US citizens need an eVisa for Brazil. Day 12 (Brazilian-side falls) requires it. Apply weeks in advance. If the visa won't process in time, drop the Brazilian-side day and add a helicopter tour over the falls or a return Devil's Throat morning visit.

Indonesia

Indonesia

Bali in dry season plus a Komodo liveaboard. Ubud rice terraces, Uluwatu cliffs, snorkeling and dragons in the Komodo archipelago. ~80–85°F. Peak season warning: Bali in July is busy — book restaurants (Locavore, Mama San) months ahead.

Thursday: Fly SAN → Bali (~24h)

  • ANA or Singapore Airlines
  • Lose Friday to time zones

Friday: Land Bali, transfer to Ubud

  • Land Denpasar evening
  • Drive to Ubud (~1.5h)
  • Como Uma Ubud or Mandapa

Saturday: Explore Ubud

  • Tegallalang rice terraces walk

  • Dinner at Locavore

Sunday: Tirta Empul + cooking class

  • Balinese cooking class with market visit

  • Bulgari Resort or Six Senses
  • Single Fin sunset cocktails
  • Pura Luhur Uluwatu cliff temple

Tuesday: Uluwatu beach day

  • Padang Padang or Bingin
  • Lunch at Suka Espresso
  • Resort spa

Wednesday: Fly to Komodo (~1.5h) — board liveaboard

  • Denpasar → Labuan Bajo flight
  • Board Plataran or Aliikai (4-night charter)
  • Sunset at Kalong Island bat colony

Thursday: Liveaboard — Padar + Pink Beach

  • Pink Beach snorkel
  • Manta Point if currents allow (often skipped if conditions are unsafe — flag this with the operator)

Friday: Liveaboard — Komodo dragons

Saturday: Liveaboard — Rinca Island

  • Spa afternoon
  • Resort sunset

Tuesday: Fly home from Bali (~24h)

  • Spa morning
  • Late checkout
  • Overnight

Portugal

Portugal

Lisbon old neighborhoods + Sintra day trip + a volcanic stop in the Azores + Porto and the Douro. 8 days, mostly walkable. ~70–80°F.

Sunday: Lisbon — Alfama

  • Memmo Alfama (terrace pool with Tagus view)
  • Castelo de São Jorge for the city panorama
  • Tram 28 from Martim Moniz
  • Seafood dinner at Cervejaria Ramiro
  • Ginjinha nightcap at A Ginjinha

Monday: Lisbon — Belém

  • Jerónimos Monastery (Manueline cloister)
  • Belém Tower
  • Pastéis de Belém — the original since 1837
  • MAAT museum on the waterfront
  • Dinner at Bairro do Avillez

Tuesday: Sintra day trip

  • Train to Sintra (~45 min)
  • Pena Palace (the colorful one)
  • Quinta da Regaleira initiation well
  • Castelo dos Mouros if time allows
  • Lunch in old-town Sintra

Tuesday night: Fly to Ponta Delgada

  • Evening flight Lisbon → Ponta Delgada (~2.5h)
  • Late check-in, easy night

Wednesday: Sete Cidades + Furnas

  • Furnas Boutique Hotel (or Octant Furnas)
  • Vista do Rei viewpoint over Sete Cidades twin lakes
  • Kayak or hike between the green and blue lakes
  • Soak in the iron-water thermal pool at Terra Nostra Garden
  • Cozido nas Furnas (stew cooked in the volcanic ground) at Tony's

Thursday: Lagoa do Fogo + Caldeira Velha

  • Hike down to Lagoa do Fogo crater lake (~2h round trip)
  • Soak at Caldeira Velha thermal waterfall pools
  • Arruda pineapple plantation tour on the way back
  • Optional: whale + dolphin trip from Ponta Delgada with Futurismo

Friday: Fly to Porto

  • Flight Ponta Delgada → Porto (~2.5h)
  • The Yeatman (Michelin-star, port-themed, river views) or Maison Albar Le Monumental Palace
  • Walk Ribeira waterfront and cross Dom Luís I Bridge at sunset
  • Livraria Lello bookshop
  • Dinner at Cantinho do Avillez or DOP

Saturday: Douro Valley

  • Drive or train to Pinhão (~2h)
  • Rabelo boat cruise on the Douro
  • Lunch at DOC by the river
  • Tasting at Quinta do Crasto or Quinta Nova
  • Back to Porto, last port at Vinologia
