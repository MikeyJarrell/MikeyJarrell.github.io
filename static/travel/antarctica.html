Antarctica

Small-ship Antarctic Peninsula expedition — penguins, whales, glacier kayaking.

Stops: Ushuaia · Drake Passage · Antarctic Peninsula · Lemaire Channel

Temp: 25–45°F, austral summer

Day 1: Fly SAN → Buenos Aires

  • Overnight flight

Day 2: Connect BA → Ushuaia

  • ~3.5h south to the end of the world
  • Walk the Beagle Channel waterfront
  • Check in at Arakur Resort

Day 3: Embark in Ushuaia

  • Board the Quark Ultramarine or similar small expedition vessel
  • Sail through the Beagle Channel at dusk

Days 4–5: Drake Passage crossing

  • Two days at sea
  • Lectures from the expedition team
  • First icebergs by day 5

Day 6: South Shetland Islands

  • Zodiac landing on Half Moon Island or Aitcho
  • Chinstrap penguin colonies

Day 7: Antarctic Peninsula — Neko Harbour

  • Continental landing on mainland Antarctica
  • Glacier-front kayaking
  • Watch humpback whales bubble-net feeding

Day 8: Lemaire Channel + Petermann Island

  • Sail through the narrowest channel of the trip
  • Gentoo penguin colonies, leopard seals

Day 9: Paradise Bay

  • Polar plunge (optional but mandatory bragging rights)
  • Argentinian station visit if it's open

Day 10: Deception Island

  • Volcanic caldera you sail into
  • Walk on a black-sand beach with steaming geothermal vents

Days 11–12: Drake return

  • Two days back across, often rougher than southbound

Day 13: Disembark in Ushuaia, fly to BA

  • Last steak dinner in Buenos Aires

Day 14: Fly home

  • Overnight to SAN
