Iceland

A clockwise Ring Road drive — waterfalls, glaciers, geothermal pools, and the highlands.

Stops: Reykjavík · Vík · Höfn · Mývatn · Snæfellsnes · Landmannalaugar

Temp: 55–65°F, endless daylight

Endless daylight and zero humidity. Ring Road drive, Reynisfjara black sands, Jökulsárlón glacier lagoon, Sky Lagoon soak. ~55–65°F all month.

Day 1: Fly SAN → Reykjavík (~12h)

  • Red-eye to Keflavík
  • Pick up rental at the airport

Day 2: Reykjavík + Sky Lagoon

  • 7-step Sky Lagoon ritual to fight jet lag
  • Walk Reykjavík harbor
  • Dinner at Matur og Drykkur

Day 3: Drive the Golden Circle

  • Þingvellir, Geysir, Gullfoss
  • Soak at Secret Lagoon
  • Overnight in Hella

Day 4: Drive south coast (~3h)

StayHotel Kría

  • Seljalandsfoss + Skógafoss
  • Reynisfjara black-sand beach

Day 5: Skaftafell + Jökulsárlón

StayHali Country Hotel or Fosshotel Glacier Lagoon

  • Skaftafell Svartifoss loop (~2mi flat)
  • Jökulsárlón glacier lagoon boat ride
  • Diamond Beach

Day 6: Drive east to Egilsstaðir (~5h)

StayHali Country Hotel or Fosshotel Glacier Lagoon

  • Stokksnes / Vestrahorn stop
  • Long scenic drive along the east fjords
  • Overnight Egilsstaðir

Day 7: Mývatn area

StayHali Country Hotel or Fosshotel Glacier Lagoon

  • Mývatn Nature Baths
  • Krafla geothermal area + Goðafoss
  • Overnight Akureyri

Day 8: Drive to Snæfellsnes (~5h)

StayHotel Búðir or Stykkishólmur

  • Forest Lagoon morning soak
  • Long drive south

Day 9: Snæfellsnes peninsula

StayHotel Búðir or Stykkishólmur

  • Kirkjufell viewpoint
  • Arnarstapi cliff walk (~2mi)
  • Búðakirkja black church

Day 10: Drive back to Reykjavík (~3h) + Glymur hike

StayHotel Búðir or Stykkishólmur

  • Glymur waterfall hike (~2-3h, second-tallest in Iceland)
  • Borgarnes Settlement Center stop
  • Lunch at Settlement Café

Day 11: Explore Reykjavík

StayHotel Búðir or Stykkishólmur

  • Hallgrímskirkja tower
  • Harpa concert hall
  • Fish and chips at Reykjavík Fish

Day 12: Landmannalaugar super-jeep day

StayHotel Búðir or Stykkishólmur

  • Full-day super-jeep into the highlands (~10-12h)
  • Rhyolite mountains, hot-river soak at Laugavegur trailhead
  • Terrain only accessible by 4WD; pure highland landscape
  • Whale watching from Reykjavík is the rest-day alternative if the jeep tour feels too much

Day 13: Blue Lagoon, fly home

  • Premium booking with Lava Restaurant lunch
  • Drop rental at Keflavík
