Ethiopia

Lalibela rock churches, Simien gelada baboons, and the Danakil sulfur fields.

Stops: Addis Ababa · Lalibela · Gondar · Simien Mountains · Danakil Depression

Temp: 60–95°F (varies dramatically)

Day 1: Fly SAN → Addis Ababa (~22h via Frankfurt or Dubai)

StaySheraton Addis or Skylight Hotel

  • Tej (honey wine) at Yod Abyssinia

Day 2: Addis Ababa

StaySheraton Addis or Skylight Hotel

  • National Museum to see Lucy fossil
  • Holy Trinity Cathedral
  • Mercato — Africa's largest open-air market

Day 3: Fly to Lalibela

StaySheraton Addis or Skylight Hotel

  • ~1h flight
  • Mountain View Hotel
  • Afternoon at Bet Medhane Alem

Day 4: Lalibela — rock-hewn churches

StaySheraton Addis or Skylight Hotel

  • Eleven 12th-century churches carved into the bedrock
  • Bet Giyorgis (the cross-shaped one) at dawn
  • Saturday market if timed

Day 5: Fly to Gondar

StaySheraton Addis or Skylight Hotel

  • Royal Enclosure of Fasil Ghebbi
  • Debre Berhan Selassie ceiling angels

Day 6: Drive to Simien Mountains (~3h)

StayLimalimo Lodge

  • Walk among gelada baboons

Day 7: Simien — full day hike

StayLimalimo Lodge

  • Hike to Geech viewpoint
  • Walia ibex sightings on the escarpment

Day 8: Fly back to Addis, on to Mekele

StayLimalimo Lodge

  • Connection at Bole airport
  • Drive to base of the Danakil Depression

Day 9: Danakil — Erta Ale volcano

StayLimalimo Lodge

  • 4WD across the salt flats
  • Sunset ascent to the lava lake at Erta Ale
  • Sleep on the rim

Day 10: Dallol — sulfur springs

StayLimalimo Lodge

  • Multicolored geothermal landscapes
  • Hottest inhabited place on Earth (~95°F average)

Day 11: Fly back to Addis

StayLimalimo Lodge

  • Last meal at Yod Abyssinia
  • Coffee ceremony

Day 12: Fly home
